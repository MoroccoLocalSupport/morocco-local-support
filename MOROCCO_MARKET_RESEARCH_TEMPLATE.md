# Morocco Market Research Template

This template is used to structure basic market research requests related to Morocco.

It is designed for foreign clients, entrepreneurs, buyers, small businesses and companies exploring the Moroccan market.

---

## 1. Client Objective

**Main objective:**  
Describe what the client wants to understand about the Moroccan market.

Examples:

- Product demand in Morocco
- Local competitors
- Price levels
- Supplier availability
- Distribution channels
- Consumer behavior
- Business opportunities
- Market entry preparation

---

## 2. Target Sector in Morocco

**Industry or sector:**  
Add the relevant market sector.

Examples:

- Tourism
- Agriculture
- Food products
- Clothing
- Cosmetics
- Real estate
- Digital services
- Logistics
- Retail
- Construction
- Education
- Import/export

---

## 3. Target City or Region

Market information in Morocco may differ by city or region.

Possible locations:

- Casablanca
- Rabat
- Marrakech
- Tangier
- Fes
- Agadir
- Meknes
- Oujda
- Laayoune
- Other Moroccan cities or regions

---

## 4. Information to Collect

The market research may include:

- Local prices
- Competitors
- Product availability
- Supplier list
- Customer behavior
- Distribution channels
- Local demand indicators
- Public business information
- Market opportunities
- Practical risks
- Local contacts when available

---

## 5. Competitor Research

For each competitor, collect:

- Business name
- Location
- Website or social media
- Products or services
- Price range
- Customer positioning
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Public reviews if available
- Communication quality

---

## 6. Price Comparison

Price research may include:

- Retail price
- Wholesale price
- Supplier price
- Delivery cost
- Minimum order quantity
- City-based price difference
- Seasonal variation
- Payment conditions

---

## 7. Supplier or Partner Research

For supplier or partner search in Morocco, collect:

- Business name
- Contact information
- City
- Product or service offered
- Public presence
- Communication response
- Initial credibility indicators
- Price or offer details
- Notes and recommendations

---

## 8. Market Entry Notes

A basic Morocco market entry summary may include:

- Opportunities
- Main challenges
- Local habits
- Pricing expectations
- Distribution options
- Possible partners
- Practical next steps
- Need for field verification or local coordination

---

## 9. Final Summary

The final research summary should be clear, practical and decision-oriented.

It may include:

- Key findings
- Useful contacts
- Price comparison
- Market signals
- Risk indicators
- Recommendations
- Suggested next actions

---
