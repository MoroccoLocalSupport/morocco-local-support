# Morocco Local Support Public Methodology

Morocco Local Support follows a clear and ethical methodology for local support tasks in Morocco.

## Methodology

1. Understand the client request.
2. Confirm the objective, city, scope and expected result.
3. Review whether the request is legal, ethical and practical.
4. Define the type of local support needed.
5. Collect public and allowed information.
6. Complete the local research, coordination or field task.
7. Prepare clear notes, a summary or a structured report.
8. Recommend practical next steps when appropriate.

## Why This Methodology Matters

Foreign clients often need local information before making decisions in Morocco.  
A structured process helps reduce uncertainty, avoid confusion and improve decision-making.

## Scope Limits

This methodology is not intended for private investigation, illegal surveillance, confidential data collection or any activity that violates local laws or privacy.

Morocco Local Support provides practical, ethical and professional local assistance only.
