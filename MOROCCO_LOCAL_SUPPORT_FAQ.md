# Morocco Local Support FAQ

## What is Morocco Local Support?

Morocco Local Support is a local assistance service that helps foreign clients, businesses, buyers, travelers and entrepreneurs with reliable on-the-ground support in Morocco.

## What services are available?

Services may include field verification, supplier search, market research, business visit support, local coordination, calls and follow-ups, product price comparison and structured reporting.

## Can you verify a supplier in Morocco?

Morocco Local Support can help with practical supplier verification steps such as address checks, local presence observation, supplier communication, public information review and structured summaries.

## Can you visit a location in Morocco?

A location visit may be possible depending on the city, feasibility, legal limits and privacy considerations.

## Do you provide photos or videos?

Photos or videos may be provided when allowed, legal, reasonable and respectful of privacy.

## Do you guarantee the reliability of a supplier?

No. Morocco Local Support does not guarantee future behavior, product quality or business outcomes. The service helps reduce uncertainty by providing practical local information and structured observations.

## Do you provide private investigation?

No. Morocco Local Support does not provide private investigation, spying, illegal surveillance, bribery, impersonation or confidential data collection.

## Who can use Morocco Local Support?

Foreign buyers, entrepreneurs, investors, import/export professionals, travelers, small businesses and companies exploring Morocco can use this service.

## How should I send a request?

Send clear details about the task, city, objective, deadline, available documents or links, and expected result.
