# Morocco Business Visit Checklist

This checklist helps foreign clients, entrepreneurs, buyers and companies prepare a professional business visit to Morocco.

A well-prepared business visit can save time, reduce uncertainty and help the client meet the right people with clear objectives.

---

## 1. Define the Purpose of the Visit

Before traveling to Morocco, clarify the main objective:

- Supplier visit
- Business meeting
- Factory or workshop visit
- Market research
- Product sourcing
- Investment exploration
- Real estate or location visit
- Partnership discussion
- Event, fair or exhibition attendance
- Custom local mission

---

## 2. Prepare Key Information

The client should prepare:

- Company or personal profile
- Business objective
- Target cities
- Names of companies or contacts to meet
- Preferred dates
- Required language support
- Expected deliverables
- Budget range
- Important questions to ask
- Documents or product references if needed

---

## 3. Morocco Local Coordination

Morocco Local Support may assist with:

- Appointment coordination
- Local calls and follow-ups
- Supplier or company contact
- Visit planning
- Practical orientation
- Local transportation coordination when needed
- Translator or local assistant coordination when possible
- Daily visit summary
- Photos or videos when allowed
- Structured report after the visit

---

## 4. Before the Meeting

Before attending a business meeting in Morocco, it is useful to confirm:

- Exact address
- Meeting time
- Contact person
- Phone number
- Language of communication
- Purpose of the meeting
- Documents to bring
- Whether photos or videos are allowed
- Expected meeting duration

---

## 5. During the Visit

Important points to observe:

- Business location
- Professional behavior
- Product availability
- Communication quality
- Price consistency
- Visible activity
- Team or staff presence
- Responsiveness
- Documents or brochures provided
- General credibility indicators

---

## 6. After the Visit

A useful post-visit summary may include:

- What was discussed
- People met
- Key information collected
- Positive signs
- Risk indicators
- Photos or videos when allowed
- Next steps
- Recommendations

---

## 7. Morocco Business Visit Risks to Consider

Foreign clients should be careful with:

- Unclear meeting objectives
- Last-minute changes
- Unrealistic promises
- Pressure to pay quickly
- Lack of written confirmation
- Inconsistent business information
- Unverified addresses
- No clear follow-up process
